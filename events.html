Haji Lane Block Party

Get Ready for Kampong Glam Precinct Party, where Haji Lane will be transformed into an open-air party venue with a legendary line-up. It's FREE! First 200 attendees will receive a free tequila shot!
Enjoy tracks from DJ Titus Singam and DJ Shaifudin Suhaimi, and music from bands Havana Social Club and Fungkimunkees.
There’ll be Latin, 90s R&B, Hip Hop, and Top 40’s music, plus tequilas, margaritas, lucky draw prizes and face painting for the kiddos, too!


Organised by: One Kampong Gelam
Official Sponsors: SINGTEL DASH , GPSS 2019
Event Date : 21 September 2019 Saturday
Event Time : 5:00pm - 11:30pm
Venue : Piedra Negra, 241 Beach Road Singapore 189753
